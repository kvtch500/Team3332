Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information TEAM 3332 ("we," "us") collects when you use team3332.com and the TEAM 3332 mobile and web apps (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this simple: we collect only what the Service needs to work, and we never sell your personal data or use your location for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password. Passwords are stored only as secure cryptographic hashes — we cannot see them.
  • Profile information you choose to add: bio, location, avatar image, pace group, and membership tier.
  • Precise location (GPS) data: when you start recording a run, the app collects your device's precise location to map your route and calculate distance, pace, and duration. With your permission, this collection continues in the background — while the app is minimized or your screen is locked — for the duration of that run, so your run keeps tracking while the phone is in your pocket. We collect location only during an active recording session; we do not track your location when you are not recording a run. Raw location points are filtered on your device for accuracy, and the resulting route and run summary are saved to your account.
  • Activity data: runs you log, including distance, pace, duration, calories, notes, and route data.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by Stripe. We never receive or store your card number — we store only a Stripe customer reference and your subscription status.
  • Technical basics: standard server logs (IP address, request times) used for security and keeping the Service running.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To record and map your runs and calculate distance, pace, duration, and calories.
  • To operate your account, memberships, leaderboards, challenges, badges, and group runs.
  • To process payments and manage renewals through Stripe.
  • To send service emails — welcome messages, password resets, and important account or billing notices — via Resend, our email provider.
  • To keep the Service secure and prevent abuse.

We use your location data solely to provide the run-tracking features you request. We do not use location for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to recording your runs, and we never sell it.

3. Location Permissions & Your Control

The app asks for your permission before accessing location, and again before tracking in the background. You can grant, limit, or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → TEAM 3332; Android: Settings → Apps → TEAM 3332 → Permissions → Location). Revoking location permission disables run tracking but does not otherwise affect your account.

4. What Other Members Can See

TEAM 3332 is a team. Your name, avatar, pace group, badges, and logged activities (such as distance and pace) are visible to other members on leaderboards, challenges, and team feeds. Your precise route map is shown only where you choose to share it, and your email address and payment details are never visible to other members.

5. Who We Share Data With

We share data only with the service providers that run the platform, and only what each needs to do its job:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Railway — application and database hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Mapbox and OpenStreetMap — map rendering. To display the map behind your route, the app sends the map provider the coordinates needed to fetch the surrounding map imagery. These providers process that data under their own privacy policies and do not receive your account identity.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our members. We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.

6. Mobile Apps & App Stores

The TEAM 3332 app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play. When you download or use the app, those platforms may collect information under their own privacy policies, which we do not control. On your device, the app stores a login token and your in-progress run data locally so the Service works reliably and offline.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

The Service stores a login token on your device so you stay signed in. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account and personal data at any time directly in the app under Account → Delete Account, or by emailing ernest@team3332.com. We will delete your data within 30 days, except for records we must keep for legal or accounting purposes (such as payment records).

9. Security

All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), passwords are hashed with bcrypt, and access to production systems is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow standard best practices to protect your data.

10. Children

The Service is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

11. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your personal information at any time — most profile data can be edited directly in the app, and anything else can be requested by email. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under local privacy laws; we honor reasonable requests regardless of jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or in-app notice before they take effect.

13. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: ernest@team3332.com.
