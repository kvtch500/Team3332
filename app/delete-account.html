Delete Your TEAM 3332 Account

Katch Media LLC · TEAM 3332 app and team3332.com

You can request deletion of your TEAM 3332 account and its associated data at any time. This page explains how, and exactly what happens to your data.

How to Request Deletion

  • Email ernest@team3332.com with the subject line "Delete my TEAM 3332 account", sent from the email address on your account.
  • We will confirm the request and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account (name, email address, password hash, profile photo, bio, pace group)
  • All recorded activities — runs, walks, routes, distances, pace, heart-rate data
  • Your leaderboard entries, badges, and challenge history

What May Be Kept

  • Payment records required for legal, tax, and accounting purposes (kept by us and Stripe for the legally required retention period)
  • Server logs containing no profile information, which expire on a rolling basis

Partial Deletion

If you want specific data removed without closing your account — for example deleting individual activities — you can delete activities directly in the app, or email us at the address above and we'll handle it.

Questions

Anything unclear, email ernest@team3332.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
