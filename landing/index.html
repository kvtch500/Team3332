How It Works

From Sign-Up to Team.

Getting started takes less than 5 minutes. Growing takes however far you want to go.

1

Join the Team

Sign up, pick your membership tier, and complete your runner profile with pace group and goals.

2

Connect & Track

Link your GPS watch, phone, or Strava account. Log every run and see it reflected in real time on your team feed.

3

Compete & Connect

Join monthly challenges, climb pace-group leaderboards, and engage with your captain and fellow members.

4

Earn & Level Up

Hit milestones to earn badges, unlock perks, and climb toward a Captain designation with your own challenges.

Become a Captain

The Captain badge is earned — not bought. Through consistent performance, qualifying runs, and community leadership, standout members rise to guide others and shape the culture of TEAM 3332.
