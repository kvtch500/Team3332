# TEAM 3332 — App Summary

## What It Is

TEAM 3332 is a virtual running team — not a tracking app, not a social feed, but an actual team you belong to.

Every major running platform right now gives you data. Strava shows your miles. Nike Run Club gives you training plans. Garmin logs your stats. But none of them give you a team. You're still running alone, just with an audience.

## What's Missing in the Running Community

What's missing in the running community is identity and structure. Local run clubs exist, but they're geography-dependent, often intimidating for slower runners, and have no real progression system. There's no team you can join from anywhere in the world, get placed in a pace group with people at your level, earn your way up, and have a captain who actually mentors you.

## How TEAM 3332 Fills the Gap

TEAM 3332 fills that gap. It takes the best parts of a real sports team — ranks, captains, group challenges, progression — and makes it virtual and inclusive. A runner in Atlanta and a runner in London can be on the same team, compete on the same leaderboard, and push each other through the same challenge.

The annual membership model matters too. It creates skin in the game. People who pay $199 to be part of something show up differently than people who downloaded a free app. That commitment is what turns a platform into a community.

## The Bottom Line

The running world has plenty of tools. It doesn't have a home. That's what 3332 is.
